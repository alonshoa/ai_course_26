  <h1>Artificial Intelligence & Computer Science</h1>
  <p>
    Welcome to a year-long journey through creative coding, data thinking, and responsible AI. You will build foundations in programming and mathematics, learn how modern machine learning models work, and practice communicating insights with visualizations and notebooks.
  </p>
  <div class="callout">
    <strong>What you'll master:</strong> Python for data, PyTorch experimentation, model training, ethical thinking, collaborative GitHub workflows, and the confidence to ship projects that solve real problems.

<section id="toc" style="margin-top: 2rem;">
  <h2>Course Structure</h2>
  <div class="grid two-col">
    <article class="card">
      <h3>Foundations</h3>
      <ul class="list">
        <li>Introduction to AI & ML</li>
        <li>Working with Colab and GitHub</li>
        <li>Python for Data (functions, control flow, best practices)</li>
        <li>Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib</li>
      </ul>
    </article>
    <article class="card">
      <h3>Models & Training</h3>
      <ul class="list">
        <li>Machine Learning Basics + evaluation</li>
        <li>Classification problems & datasets</li>
        <li>Linear regression & cost functions (MSE, BCE)</li>
        <li>Softmax & multi-class thinking</li>
        <li>Perceptron and fully-connected networks</li>
        <li>Forward & backward propagation with PyTorch</li>
        <li>Model tuning, regularization, validation</li>
      </ul>
    </article>
    <article class="card">
      <h3>Visualization & Communication</h3>
      <ul class="list">
        <li>Plotting best practices and color-blind safe palettes</li>
        <li>Interactive demos and dashboards</li>
        <li>Storytelling with notebooks</li>
      </ul>
    </article>
    <article class="card">
      <h3>Projects & Impact</h3>
      <ul class="list">
        <li>Project ideation and scoping</li>
        <li>Data ethics and responsible AI</li>
        <li>Final project guidelines and checkpoints</li>
      </ul>
    </article>
  </div>
</section>
