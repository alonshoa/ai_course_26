  <h1>Final Project Launchpad</h1>
  <p>Design, train, and communicate an AI project that matters to you. You will propose an idea, ship iterative prototypes, and present your findings with visuals and a clear narrative.</p>

  <h2>Milestones</h2>
  <ul class="list">
    <li><strong>Proposal:</strong> Define the problem, data source, and expected impact.</li>
    <li><strong>Prototype:</strong> Build a baseline model with clear metrics.</li>
    <li><strong>Experimentation:</strong> Compare models, adjust hyperparameters, and track results.</li>
    <li><strong>Storytelling:</strong> Visualize outcomes with color-blind-safe charts and concise explanations.</li>
    <li><strong>Presentation:</strong> Demo, slides, and a short report hosted on this site.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Rubric Highlights</h2>
  <ul class="list">
    <li>Technical correctness and reproducibility</li>
    <li>Thoughtful evaluation and error analysis</li>
    <li>Responsible AI considerations</li>
    <li>Clarity of communication and visuals</li>
    <li>Project management and version control hygiene</li>
  </ul>
</section>
